# Supplier Logic & Shipping Thresholds

## S&S Activewear
- **Free Shipping:** Orders over $200.
- **Cutoff Time:** 4:00 PM EST.
- **Best For:** Hats (Valucap), Bella+Canvas, Next Level.
- **API:** Active.

## AS Colour
- **Free Shipping:** Orders over $500.
- **Best For:** Premium Tees (Staple, Classic), Hoodies (Relax, Stencil).
- **Notes:** High quality, consistent sizing.

## SanMar
- **Free Shipping:** Orders over $200.
- **Best For:** Port Authority, Sport-Tek, Nike.
